The Founder Aditi Malik

From a Banker at Morgan Stanley to an Entrepreneur, Aditi Malik has innovated through some of the greatest challenges of the global economy in the last 29 years.
A high performing strategic thinker, she has 19 years of experience in the banking and financial services industry. She specialises in business strategy, organisational development, operations, sales, relationship management and marketing & branding.

Driven by her untethered zeal for self-exploration and self-mastery, Aditi's transition into the world of learning and development was a natural evolution. Taking her 13 years of experience to L&D, she served as the Head of Business Development and Chief Talent Officer at Morgan Stanley. She has spent 16 years enabling people on the path of self-discovery.
Today, Aditi is an executive coach and a consultant for industry leaders. She leads organizations with end-to-end learning and development solutions. Arohan is the culmination of Aditi's 20+ year journey in the corporate world. In her drive for excellence, she has undertaken workshops and certifications internationally. She is also an integral part of a global initiative on culture change for a leading international bank across the Asia Pacific Region in Australia, New Zealand and India.
When she is not helping people evolve into the best versions of themselves, Aditi can be found reading, travelling or enjoying music.
